Our Approach

From plant to extract, every alkaloid preserved.

Most botanical extracts sacrifice compound integrity for yield. High heat, aggressive solvents, and rush-processing destroy the very alkaloids consumers are seeking.

Our cold-process extraction preserves the full alkaloid profile -- mitragynine, 7-hydroxymitragynine, kavalactones, aporphines -- exactly as nature intended.

  • Single-origin plant material with full traceability
  • Third-party COA for every batch
  • GMP-compliant facility in Texas
  • Heavy metal and microbial screening
  • Alkaloid content verified by HPLC
