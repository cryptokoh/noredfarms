About Nored Farms

Bridging ancient botanical wisdom with modern extraction science. From our roots in Texas Hill Country, we craft the highest-quality ethnobotanical extracts available anywhere.

Our Story

Nored Farms was founded in the rolling hills of Texas Hill Country, born from a deep curiosity about the plants that have shaped human culture for millennia. What began as personal research into ethnobotanicals more than 15 years ago has grown into a dedicated operation focused on extraction science, transparency, and education.

Our journey started with a simple question: what happens when you combine ancient botanical knowledge with rigorous modern science? The answer led us down a path of studying traditional plant use across cultures, mastering extraction methodologies, and building laboratory processes that honor both the plant and the person who will use it.

Today, Nored Farms operates from a GMP-compliant facility where every batch is crafted with precision and tested by independent third-party laboratories. We are not chasing trends. We are building a body of work rooted in evidence, patience, and respect for the natural world.

Our Mission

We believe the botanical industry deserves better. Too many companies hide behind vague labels and unverified claims. At Nored Farms, we take a different approach: full transparency in sourcing, rigorous third-party testing, and a commitment to education over hype.

Our mission is to make high-quality botanical extracts accessible while raising the standard for what consumers should expect. Every product we release comes with clear documentation of its origins, extraction method, and lab results. We want you to know exactly what you are getting and why it matters.

Education is at the center of everything we do. Through our classes, research articles, and course platform, we share the science behind these plants so that our community can make informed decisions for themselves.
